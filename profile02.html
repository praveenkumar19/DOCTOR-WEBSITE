Meet Dr. Praveen Kumar

Dr. Praveen Kumar is a Senior Interventional Cardiologist at the prestigious Dr. Rela Institute with over 15 years of clinical experience. He is renowned for his expertise in complex coronary interventions, advanced heart failure management, and heart transplantation. His dual background in pediatrics and cardiology gives him a unique perspective on congenital and acquired heart diseases across all ages.

Education & fellowships

  • MBBS – Stanley Medical College, Chennai (2005)
  • MD Pediatrics – Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research, Chandigarh (2009)
  • DM Cardiology – Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute, Trivandrum (2014)
  • Advanced heart failure & transplant fellowship – Harefield Hospital, UK (2017)

Core expertise

Dr. Kumar's practice spans interventional cardiology, mechanical circulatory support, and heart transplant medicine. He has performed over 2,000+ coronary interventions and is part of the core team that established the heart transplant program at Dr. Rela Institute.

🔹 Complex high-risk angioplasty 🔹 Heart failure devices (CRT, LVAD) 🔹 Heart transplant recipient care 🔹 Pulmonary hypertension 🔹 Paediatric-to-adult transition

Dr. Rela Institute

As a senior consultant in the Department of Cardiology and Heart Transplantation, Dr. Praveen Kumar works closely with a multidisciplinary team including cardiac surgeons, intensivists, and transplant coordinators. He is instrumental in pre‑transplant optimization, post‑transplant immunosuppression, and long‑term graft surveillance. The institute is one of the few in India with a dedicated heart failure and transplant unit.
