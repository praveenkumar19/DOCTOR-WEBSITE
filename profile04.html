Meet Dr. Venkatasaaarathy

Dr. Venkatasaaarathy is a distinguished Pediatric Cardiologist with over 13 years of dedicated experience in diagnosing and managing heart conditions in children — from fetuses to adolescents. He currently serves as a senior consultant at Colorful Children's Hospital & Apollo Clinic, where he leads the fetal cardiology and postnatal cardiac care programs. His empathetic approach and clinical precision have made him a trusted name among families dealing with congenital and acquired heart disease.

Education & fellowships

  • MBBS – Madras Medical College, Chennai (2008)
  • MD Pediatrics – All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi (2012)
  • FNB Pediatric Cardiology – Narayana Health, Bengaluru (2016)
  • Advanced fetal echocardiography – Great Ormond Street Hospital, London (2019)

Core expertise

Dr. Venkatasaaarathy's clinical practice covers the entire spectrum of paediatric cardiology, with special focus on early intervention and family-centred care. He has performed over 1,500+ echocardiograms in neonates and is skilled in both non-invasive and invasive cardiac procedures for children.

🔹 Fetal echocardiography (from 16 weeks) 🔹 Neonatal cardiac ICU 🔹 Device closure (ASD, VSD, PDA) 🔹 Kawasaki disease follow‑up 🔹 Heart failure in children

Colorful Children's Hospital & Apollo Clinic

At this dedicated paediatric centre, Dr. Venkatasaaarathy works within a multidisciplinary team that includes paediatric cardiac surgeons, intensivists, and fetal medicine specialists. Together they run a comprehensive congenital heart program offering prenatal counselling, surgical correction, and long‑term follow‑up. The institution is known for its child‑friendly environment and high success rates in complex neonatal cardiac surgery.

Care philosophy
