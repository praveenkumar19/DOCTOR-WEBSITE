Meet Dr. John Raj

Dr. John Raj is a distinguished Interventional Cardiologist with over 17 years of dedicated experience in managing complex coronary cases. Based at the renowned MIOT International, he has earned a reputation for precision, empathy, and innovation in cardiac care. His patient-first approach combined with advanced technical skills makes him one of the most sought-after cardiologists in the region.

Qualifications & training

  • MBBS – Madras Medical College (Gold medalist)
  • DM Cardiology – Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences
  • FACC – Fellow of the American College of Cardiology
  • Advanced interventional fellowship in complex coronary interventions (Netherlands, 2014)

Clinical expertise

Dr. Raj specialises in high-risk angioplasty, rotational atherectomy, and complex bifurcation stenting. He has successfully performed over 3,500+ coronary interventions with outstanding outcomes. His niche interests include:

🔹 Primary angioplasty 🔹 Drug-eluting stents 🔹 IVUS & OCT guided procedures 🔹 Critical care cardiology

MIOT International

As a senior consultant at MIOT International, Dr. John Raj leads the interventional team for emergency and elective procedures. MIOT's state-of-the-art cath labs and heart command centre allow him to deliver world-class care — from emergency primary angioplasty to complex multi‑vessel interventions. He also mentors young fellows and contributes to institutional research.

Approach & philosophy

"Every heartbeat matters. I believe in treating the person, not just the blockage. Clear communication with patients and families is part of the healing." His holistic methodology includes preventive counselling, lifestyle modification, and shared decision‑making.
