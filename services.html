Echocardiography: Complete Heart Ultrasound

Echocardiography uses sound waves to create detailed images of your heart's structure and function. This non-invasive procedure helps detect heart abnormalities, valve problems, and fluid around the heart.

Duration

30-45 minutes

Pain Level

Painless

Cost

₹2,500 - ₹4,500

Key Benefits:

  • Non-invasive, no radiation
  • Real-time heart imaging
  • Detects valve problems
  • Assesses heart pumping strength
  • No preparation needed
  • Safe for all ages

Frequently Asked Questions:

Do I need to prepare for this test?

No special preparation is needed. You can eat and drink normally before the test.

How long does it take?

The test typically takes 30-45 minutes. You can return to normal activities immediately after.

Dobutamine Stress Test: Pharmacological Heart Evaluation

This test uses medication to simulate exercise and evaluate how your heart responds to stress. It's ideal for patients who cannot exercise on a treadmill due to physical limitations.

Duration

60-90 minutes

Monitoring

Continuous ECG

Cost

₹4,500 - ₹7,000

Key Benefits:

  • For patients unable to exercise
  • Detects blocked arteries
  • Assesses heart function under stress
  • Safe and controlled environment
  • Continuous monitoring
  • Accurate results

Frequently Asked Questions:

What does the medication feel like?

You may feel your heart beating faster, similar to exercise. Some patients feel warm or flushed.

Are there any side effects?

Side effects are mild and temporary: slight headache, palpitations, or chest discomfort that resolves quickly.

Coronary Angioplasty & Bypass Surgery

How long is recovery?

Angioplasty: 1 week; Bypass: 6-12 weeks for full recovery.

Will I need medication after?

Yes, blood thinners and other medications are typically prescribed to prevent future blockages.

ECG & Treadmill Stress Test

Resting ECG records your heart's electrical activity. Stress test monitors heart function while you walk on a treadmill to detect exercise-induced abnormalities.

Duration

30-60 minutes

Exercise

Treadmill walking

Cost

₹1,500 - ₹3,000

Key Benefits:

  • Detects irregular heartbeats
  • Identifies blocked arteries
  • Assesses exercise tolerance
  • Non-invasive
  • Quick results
  • Monitors treatment progress

Frequently Asked Questions:

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes and walking shoes. Avoid heavy meals before the test.

Can I take my medications?

Consult your doctor - some heart medications may need to be paused before the test.
